Sweet Potato Dish

Description

This sweet potato dish is very healthy, with of course the sweet potato, chicken, onion, bell pepper and a creme fraiche sauce. It also takes a short time to make, so let's get going!

Ingredients

  • 400 grams of ready-made sweet potato cubes
  • 3 cloves of garlic
  • 400 grams of chicken breast, diced
  • 1 tablespoon oystersauce
  • 1 onion
  • 1/2 leek
  • 1 yellow bell pepper
  • 125 grams of creme fraiche
  • 75 grams of grated cheese
  • 1 tablespoon of olive oil

Preparation

  1. Preheat the oven at 160 degrees Celsius.
  2. Chop the Onion and mince the garlic.
  3. Heat a bit of olive oil in a pan and fry the onion and garlic.
  4. Dice the bell pepper and add to the onion and garlic, together with the sweet potato cubes. Fry on low heat for 15 minutes.
  5. Add the oystersauce after.
  6. In a separarte frying pan, add the olive oil and fry the chicken breast. Season with salt and pepper.
  7. Add this all to an oven tray and pour over the creme fraiche. Finally add the grated cheese.
  8. Bake in the oven for 10 minutes.
