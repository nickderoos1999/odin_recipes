Pasta Carbonara

Description

This classic italian dish is created with only eggs, guanciale, parmesan cheese, pepper and of course pasta!

Ingredients

  • 400 grams spaghetti
  • 200 grams guanciale
  • 100 grams parmesan cheese
  • 4 eggs
  • Pepper

Preparation

  1. Dice the guanciale and bake until crispy.
  2. Meanwhile, cook the spaghetti.
  3. Stir 3 eggs and 1 egg yolk. Add a good amount of pepper.
  4. Grate the parmesan cheese and add to the egg mixture.
  5. Add the pasta to the guanciale, along with a few tablespoons of pastawater.
  6. Take the pan off the heat and add the egg mixture. Make sure to keep stirring.
