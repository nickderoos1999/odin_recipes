Pasta Pesto

Description

This dish is very flavorful, and only has a few ingredients. The tomatoes give a nice extra taste to the dish, and if you want you can add rucola and pine nuts to spice it up!

Ingredients:

  • 300 grams of your preferred pasta
  • 400 grams of chicken breast, diced
  • 1 onion
  • 250ml of cooking cream
  • 250 grams of cherry tomatoes
  • 190 grams pesto
  • Olive oil

Preparation

  1. Dice the onion and fry it in a bit of olive oil.
  2. Add the diced chicken breast and fry for about 5 minutes.
  3. Meanwhile, cook the pasta.
  4. Add the pesto and the cooking cream to the chicken. Let this simmer for a few minutes.
  5. Add the pasta to the sauce.
  6. Cut the cherry tomatoes in half and add to the pasta pesto. Let it heat up for a minute.
